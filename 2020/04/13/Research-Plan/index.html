Research Plan

Tony Teng, 3/8/2020, Fountain Valley School of Colorado

Background – Why this research

    1.Two important concepts:

         a) Data Science: Data Science is a field of study that uses specific scientific process such as statistics, programming, and business to extract knowledge, patterns, or insights from different kinds of data

    2.Problem to be solved

        b)Marketing: Marketing is the study of customer need or desire in order to sell products and gain profit from them. Market segmentation is a marketing method which divides customers into different groups based on their characteristics such as old - young, high value - low value. Churn is the loss of current customers. It is very important to minimize churn because it is costly for a company lose the existing customers.

Research Questions

    Question 1: Which factors are affecting the response rate?

    We can analyze the influence of those factors through data visualizations based on ggplot2.

    Question 2: How to predict the response rate?

    We can use Machine Learning Algorithms such as Decision Tree and Random Forest to predict the customer response rate.

    Question 3: Can we segment customers into different groups?

    We can group the customers based on Customer Lifetime Value and Churn Risk through clustering.

Research Process

    1.Research process

        a)Business Understanding

            Understand the pain points of car insurance industry and the importance of data science in solving this problem

            Understand the meanings of each variable based on industry research

        b)Data Cleaning

            Use Python to clean the data to prepare for further analysis

        c)Data Visualization

            Use ggplot2 to make insightful data visualizations and understand the factors that influence the response rate.

        d)Data Modelling

            - Utilize machine learning algorithms such as Decision Tree, Random Forest to make prediction

        e)Model Evaluation

            Use 10-fold cross-validation to evaluate the performance of different algorithms

        f)Clustering

            Use K-mean clustering to segment customers into different groups based on customer churn risk and lifetime value

    2.Research tools

        a)Data Visualization:

            i.R Package - ggplot2

            ii.R Package - dplyr

        b)Data modeling

            i.Decision Tree

            ii.Random Forest

            iii.Cross Validation

        c) Clustering

            i.K-mean Clustering
